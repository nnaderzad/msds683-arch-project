## Caveats to keep the demo honest
- **Genre framing:** collection is now nationwide / all-genre (`primary_genre` is often "Other"
  or null), not strictly electronic music. Soften the recap wording, or pick the Bay-Area angle.
- **Price = latest observed face value** (mainline gold is forward-filled), not an independent
  daily tick. Don't over-claim. (PLAN.md Critical Item #7.)
- **Interest line is sparse** from snapshot `fact_trends`; use `fact_trends_daily` for a fuller chart.
- Some events appear as **near-duplicate rows** (multiple `event_id`s / dates) — pick one and stick to it.
